# Privacy Policy for Travel Buddy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction
Travel Buddy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Data:** Name, email address, phone number
- **Profile Data:** Profile picture, travel preferences, bio
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates for place recommendations and nearby features
- **Photos:** Images you upload for profile pictures and place reviews
- **Device Information:** Device ID for analytics and push notifications

### Automatically Collected
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Device type and operating system version

## How We Use Your Information

- **App Functionality:** Provide core features like trip planning, place discovery, and navigation
- **Personalization:** Recommend places and trips based on your preferences and location
- **Communication:** Send notifications about trips, deals, and app updates
- **Analytics:** Improve app performance and user experience
- **Security:** Prevent fraud and ensure account security

## Data Sharing

We share data with the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)
- Authentication and user management
- Analytics and crash reporting
- Push notifications
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google Maps Platform
- Map display and location services
- Place search and geocoding
- **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Microsoft Azure
- Backend API hosting and data storage
- **Privacy Policy:** https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

**We do not sell your personal data to third parties.**

## Data Security

- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable location services or notifications in app settings

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 30 days
- Analytics data is anonymized and retained for up to 2 years

## Children's Privacy

Travel Buddy is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
